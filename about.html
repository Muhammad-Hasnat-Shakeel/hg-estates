How We Started

From A Small Office To A Digital Platform

What began as a small property consultancy in Lahore has grown into a full digital real estate platform serving clients across Pakistan and the UAE. We built HG Estates because we believe finding a home shouldn't feel overwhelming — it should feel exciting.

Today, our team of verified agents, smart search tools, and transparent listings work together to give every client the confidence they deserve — whether they're buying their first apartment or investing in commercial real estate.
